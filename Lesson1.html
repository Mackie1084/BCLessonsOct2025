1.1 Developing Listening Skills

The Communication Process

  • Sender: the person or organization that sends the message
  • Encoding: the process of putting thoughts, ideas, or information into a symbolic form
  • Message: the information, idea, or thought that the sender wants to communicate
  • Medium: the channel through which the message is sent
  • Decoding: the process of interpreting and trying to make sense of the message
  • Receiver: the person or organization that receives the message
  • Feedback: the receiver's response to the message

1.2 Learning Non-Verbal Communication


Non-verbal communication is the process of conveying a message without the use of words. It includes facial expressions, body language, gestures, eye contact, posture, and tone of voice.

  • Includes all unwritten and unspoken messages, whether intended or not.


  • Successful communicators recognize the power of non-verbal messages
